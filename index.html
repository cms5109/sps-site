Deal Origination Analytics™ helps private equity and mezzanine investors, corporations, lenders, and advisors maximize their business development effectiveness. It is the convergence of actionable data with proprietary technology that is designed to quantify the addressable universe of a client's target market, serve as a road map to improve deal sourcing, and measure progress on a continual basis.

Best Practices for Deal Origination Analytics™
Why did XYZ Capital Miss Relevant Deals?

  • Sourcing new intermediary relationships: XYZ Capital simply does not have a relationship with the intermediary (sell-side advisor) that closed the deal in question. Perhaps there is an impression that the intermediary in question does not close any relevant deals. Perhaps the intermediary is simply new and XYZ Capital has never even heard of it. Maybe, the intermediary is aware of XYZ Capital but lacked the ability to get on your radar. Regardless, you now have the opportunity to source an entirely new relationship with an intermediary that closes relevant deals, and the information required to act on it.
  • Evaluating and improving intermediary relationships XYZ Capital has a relationship with the intermediary that closed the deal in question, but was not informed of the deal. Perhaps the intermediary correctly determined the deal was not relevant for XYZ Capital. Perhaps they were wrong. Maybe, your target criteria has simply evolved and you have been unable to seamlessly update every intermediary relationship along the way. Regardless, you are now able to independently examine every intermediary you have a relationship with, based on whether or not they are showing you the deals you want to see.

Now what should XYZ Capital do?

  • Looking to build a new relationship with an intermediary you have never heard of?There is a high likelihood we identified the professional on the relevant deal and have everything from their contact information to a short bio at your disposal.
  • Going to a conference in Chicago? Use our Portal to search for professionals at the director level or above, who closed a relevant deal in the last year, based in Chicago (the individual's location, not just the intermediary's HQ).
  • Professional-specific outreach, leveraging our interconnected database SPS is able to identify at least one intermediary professional that worked on a closed deal, 65-70% of the time.
